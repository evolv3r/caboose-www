Meet the Brewing Team

The skilled scientists and artists behind every pint.

Hector Meier

Director of Brewery Operations

A native of Houston who claims New Orleans as his home, Hector brings a diverse brewing background to the Caboose team. He's a sake aficionado but always looks forward to his favorite time of year, Oktoberfest. His blend of technical expertise and hospitality-first mindset helps keep every batch consistent, while still making room for adventurous seasonal releases.

Matt Smith

Head Brewer • Chief Barrel Aging Officer

Washington State native who started brewing when his wife gave him a home brew beer kit for his birthday. He has brewed at a number of Virginia breweries. He enjoys German Style Pilsners and Big Barrel Aged Beers. All around beer enthusiast.

Andy Bui

Brewer • Daredevil

A key member of the Caboose brewing team bringing creativity and passion to every batch. Known for pushing the envelope with unique hop combinations and playful seasonal recipes, Andy thrives on turning bold ideas into crowd-favorite pours.

Behind the Scenes

The Wort Truck & Wagon

The wort truck and wagon have been with us since the summer of 2018, right before we opened Commons. We needed a way to bring the wort (sugar water) from Caboose Tavern in Vienna, VA to Caboose Commons in Fairfax, VA to start the fermenting process in the tanks there.

The Wort Truck & Wagon were born — and have been a beloved part of the Caboose story ever since.
