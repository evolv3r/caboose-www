Coffeehouse & Breakfast Café

Located in Vienna, VA & Fairfax, VA

Specialty Coffee

Specialty Coffee at Caboose Brewing

At both locations of Caboose we are pleased to feature award winning, ethically sourced, coffee from Lone Oak Coffee Co. in Winchester, VA. Lone Oak's high quality and standards provide an excellent cup of coffee sure to please!

We've collaborated with Lone Oak to create a specialty blend only available through Caboose — and have even used it in beers of our own. Whether you're grabbing a latte on your way into work or looking for a place to work or study, Caboose wants to be your new favorite specialty coffee shop. We have both indoor and outdoor space available all day!

What We're Brewing in Our Coffee Shop

Hand Crafted Coffee

We offer coffee drinks ranging from a simple cup brewed three different ways, all the way to our specialty and seasonal beverages. Both locations rotate through offerings including refreshing iced coffees, seasonal lattes, and inventive mochas. All of our syrups are made in house.

Non-Coffee Drinks

Not a coffee drinker? We've got that covered too! From specialty iced teas to fizzy refreshers, you're sure to find something for any craving. We also offer a variety of milk options such as skim, oat, and almond.

Brewer's Favorites

Our brewery team arrives early and loves good coffee. Their go-to is usually the drip coffee. Our baristas pride themselves on quality and love to experiment — resulting in customer favorites like our Quadtado and Oatmeal Latte.

Breakfast & Brunch

Come for Coffee & Breakfast — Stay All Day!

Foodies and coffee fans alike can head to either location for coffee and a bite. Whether you want to start the day early at Caboose Commons or have a leisurely brunch at Caboose Tavern, we've got you covered!

We've got everything from breakfast sandwiches on fresh croissants, bourbon glazed French toast, to a uniquely Caboose hash. Take your breakfast to-go or stay a while with free wifi always available.
