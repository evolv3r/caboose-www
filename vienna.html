Our First Location

Award-Winning Local Microbrewery & Restaurant

Our first brewpub in Vienna, VA was originally dreamed up so that we could walk to a small-town pub, enjoy delicious food and beverage with friends, and walk back home.

You will find us tucked away in an industrial section of downtown Vienna, VA. Located right off the W&OD trail, guests can walk, run, bike, skate, or even arrive by horse to enjoy a fresh beer.

Food & Drink

Quality Sourced Restaurant

Our Vienna kitchen works with local suppliers to source quality ingredients that elevate the brewery dining experience. We feature a rotating seasonal menu that's always looking to bring something new and exciting to the table, and we offer expertly crafted coffee drinks from Lone Oak Coffee Co.

What's for Brunch?

Spend your weekend mornings with us! Our menu features classic brunch fare, breakfast sandwiches, and twists on beloved favorites. Coffee service begins at 8am Friday – Sunday.

*Please, no outside food. Outside desserts are admissible for a fee.

Private Events

Bring the Party to Caboose Tavern

Host your event in our converted industrial space. Rent The Loft or the entire restaurant. We accommodate networking happy hours, weddings, rehearsal dinners, bridal showers, baby showers, birthdays, and anniversaries.

Getting Here

Located right off the W&OD trail in downtown Vienna, VA. Guests can walk, run, bike, skate, or even arrive by horse. We are approximately 3.1 miles from the Vienna/Fairfax-GMU Metro Station. Lyft and Uber are encouraged.

Address: 520 Mill Street NE, Vienna, VA 22180
