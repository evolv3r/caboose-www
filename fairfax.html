Our Fairfax Location

Located just across from the Mosaic District, our Fairfax location is a massive three-level converted tool rental facility with one of the area's best beer gardens. Spread out on the enormous outdoor patio with a craft brew, a game of cornhole, and your favorite four-legged friend, or gather your crew inside our open space that comfortably fits groups of 2 to 200+.

Caboose Commons does not take traditional reservations. Our space is first come, first served. If you would like guaranteed space you can reserve it for a fee through our private events booking system.

2918 Eskridge Road
Fairfax, VA 22031
703-663-8833

We are cashless — please order from a QR code on your table, at the service counter, or from one of our team members.
*Our second and third levels are not wheelchair accessible.

Food

Thoughtfully-Sourced Scratch Kitchen

Caboose Commons has been a serving up favorites since 2018, offering something for everyone — from classic bar fare to crowd-pleasing options like tuna poke, blackened fish sandwich, and rotating weekly specials. It's the kind of place you'll keep coming back to.

What's for Breakfast in Fairfax?

Spend the most important meal of the day with Caboose! Our menu features house made pastries, breakfast sandwiches, and beloved favorites. We also have a full coffee menu including seasonal lattes, specialty coffee, and tea.

*Please, no outside food. Outside desserts are admissible for a flat $5 fee.

Drinks

Explore our wide selection of award winning craft beers, specialty cocktails, mocktails, and inventive coffee creations.

Bring the Party to Caboose Commons

Parking & Getting Here

We have two parking lots on site. Overflow parking can be found across the street in the Mosaic District parking garage. We are about 0.8 miles from the Dunn Loring – Merrifield Metro Station.

Address: 2918 Eskridge Road, Fairfax, VA 22031
