Mosaic District, Fairfax

Our Fairfax Location

Our Fairfax location is a massive three-level converted tool rental facility located across the street from the Mosaic District. The enormous beer garden is great for corn hole and sidewalk chalk. There is plenty of indoor and outdoor space for groups of 2 or 200+ to enjoy great beer in a fun environment.

2918 Eskridge Road
Fairfax, VA 22031
703-663-8833

We are cashless — please order from a QR code on your table, at the service counter, or from one of our team members.
*Our second and third levels are not wheelchair accessible.

Food & Coffee

Thoughtfully-Sourced Casual Dining

Caboose Commons has been a serving up favorites since 2018, offering something for everyone — from classic breakfast dishes to late-night cravings. With crowd-pleasing options like tuna poke, buffalo chicken wraps, heaping nachos, and a blackened fish sandwich, it's the kind of place you'll keep coming back to.

What's for Breakfast in Fairfax?

Spend the most important meal of the day with Caboose! Our menu features house made pastries, breakfast sandwiches, and beloved favorites. We also have a full coffee menu including seasonal lattes, specialty coffee, and tea.

*Please, no outside food. Outside desserts are admissible for a fee.

Private Events

Bring the Party to Caboose Commons

Host your event in our converted industrial loft spaces or beer garden. With our unique atmosphere and chef-driven menu, your event will be sure to leave your guests talking long after the party is over. Groups from 2 to 200+.

Parking & Getting Here

We have two parking lots on site. Overflow parking can be found across the street in the Mosaic District parking garage. We are about 0.8 miles from the Dunn Loring – Merrifield Metro Station.

Address: 2918 Eskridge Road, Fairfax, VA 22031
