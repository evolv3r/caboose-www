A Dining Experience Beyond Standard Bar Fare

Brewpub & Restaurant

Caboose Tavern – Vienna

Our Vienna kitchen works closely with suppliers to source quality ingredients that elevate the brewery dining experience. With options ranging from gourmet burgers and chicken wings to brie grilled cheese and elegant salads, there is something for every palate.

Pair it with something from the bar: award-winning beer, handcrafted cocktails, mocktails, or café drinks, there's a pour for every moment.

Dog & family friendly — pets are always welcome in our outdoor areas. Outdoor dining available year-round.

Serving brunch Friday-Sunday, 8am - 3pm

Brewpub & Restaurant

Caboose Commons – Fairfax

Our Fairfax location features a menu with something for everyone. Start with the tuna poke or nachos to share with your table, dig into our grains & greens bowl for something lighter, or go all in on a heaping burger special or the blackened fish sandwich. Whatever you're craving, there's a plate for it.

Make sure to grab something to drink, like any of our award-winning brews, expertly crafted cocktails, a refreshing non-alcoholic mocktail, or a café drink, as you enjoy your meal.

Caboose Commons has been serving up crave-worthy fare since 2018. From classic breakfast dishes to shareable bites and late-night snacks. It's the kind of menu that fits however you're spending your day here.
