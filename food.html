A Dining Experience Beyond Standard Bar Fare

Brewpub & Restaurant

Caboose Tavern – Vienna

Our Vienna kitchen works closely with suppliers to source quality ingredients that elevate the brewery dining experience. With options ranging from gourmet burgers and chicken wings to street foods and elegant salads, there is something for every palate.

Dog & family friendly — pets are always welcome in our outdoor areas. Outdoor dining available year-round.

Brewpub & Restaurant

Caboose Commons – Fairfax

Our Fairfax location features delicious meals with a global perspective. Choose from tuna poke, our grains & green bowl, a heaping turkey Rachel, nachos to share with your party, or the blackened fish sandwich. Something for everyone.

Caboose Commons has been serving up internationally inspired fare since 2018 — from classic breakfast dishes to late night snacks.
