Coffee & Breakfast

Start Your Day at Caboose

Award Winning, Locally Sourced Coffee and Beans

We're more than just a brewery! Caboose Commons opens at 7am daily and Tavern at 8am Friday through Sunday, for early birds to gather with friends for coffee or hunker down and work.

Come for Breakfast or Brunch and Stay All Day

Offerings range from homey morning classics to breakfast bowls and plates like the signature Caboose potato hash. Take your breakfast to-go or stay a while with free wifi.
